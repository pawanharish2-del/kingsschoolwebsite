About Us

Welcome to The Kings Court World School, Mansarovar–Jaipur’s only Kindergarten dedicated to a truly Inquiry-Based, World-Class Curriculum at affordable fees.

Founded with a vision to transform early childhood education in Jaipur, our Kindergarten is a nurturing haven where curiosity is celebrated, creativity is encouraged, and every child is respected as a capable learner. We combine global best practices with Indian values to create a joyful and meaningful foundation for lifelong learning.

At The Kings Court World School, we believe that every child is unique. Our experienced and passionate educators focus on holistic development—intellectual, emotional, social, and physical—ensuring that children grow with confidence and compassion.

Our campus is thoughtfully designed with Reggio-inspired learning spaces, interactive corners, natural materials, and child-led exploration zones that stimulate imagination and inquiry. Parents are active partners in this journey, making our school a warm and connected community.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: To nurture confident thinkers, compassionate individuals, and joyful learners prepared for the world ahead.

Our Methodology

Integrated Curriculum

At The Kings Court World School, Mansarovar–Jaipur, we proudly follow an Inquiry-Based, Reggio-Emilia inspired curriculum blended with Montessori principles and structured academic foundations.

Reggio Experience

  • Children viewed as strong & capable.
  • Classrooms function as the "third teacher."
  • Learning happens through projects & exploration.
  • Documentation panels showcase creativity.
  • Art, expression & open-ended materials.

Inquiry-Based Learning

  • Explore real-world concepts.
  • Discussions and hands-on investigations.
  • Critical thinking & problem-solving.
  • Communication and collaboration abilities.

Montessori Integration

  • Promotes Independence.
  • Builds Concentration.
  • Fine motor development.
  • Practical life skills integration.

Holistic Development

  • Language & Literacy.
  • Numeracy & Logical Thinking.
  • Environmental Awareness.
  • Social & Emotional Learning.
